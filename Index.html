About Us

The Diamond Generation is an initiative founded by Dr. Michael Okoli (The Entrepreneur Mentor) to empower young Nigerians aged 18 to 28.

In response to widespread unemployment, dependency culture, and technological disruption, this movement was born to build a future-ready generation.

Our goal is to raise a million young entrepreneurs who will transform the landscape and society through mentorship, discipline, and entrepreneurship.

We are shifting mindsets—from dependency to self-leadership, from waiting for opportunities to creating them.

We are not just launching a programme, we are igniting a movement. one that seeks to raise one million entrepreneurial leaders within five years. these young people the diamond generation will rewrite the narrative across enterprise, innovation, and leadership.

Who We Are. What We Stand For

Our Mission

We mentor and equip young Nigerians with the mindset, skills, and confidence to shift from dependence to self-sufficiency — rewriting the nation’s future through enterprise, leadership, and innovation.

Our Vision

A Nigeria driven by youth-powered innovation and purpose-driven leadership, where young changemakers thrive and create value for the continent and beyond.

Our Values

Empowerment, discipline, mentorship, creativity, and impact. These values spark the Diamond Generation — a force for transformation across Nigeria and beyond, fueling innovation, leadership, and lasting change.
