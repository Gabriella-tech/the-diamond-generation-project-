About Us

The Diamond Generation is an initiative founded by Dr. Michael Okoli (The Entrepreneur Mentor) to empower young Nigerians aged 18 to 28.

In response to widespread unemployment, dependency culture, and technological disruption, this movement was born to build a future-ready generation.

Our goal is to raise a million young entrepreneurs who will transform the landscape and society through mentorship, discipline, and entrepreneurship.
